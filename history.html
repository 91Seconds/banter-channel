Foundation

The founding date of the BC is currently unknown, but is speculated to have occured during 2013 which would make the channel 3 years old. It is based loosely off the Golden Age of SPC's literature, with "The Clashing Armies" and Wolf Hospital documents founding the basis of the Channel's ethical conduct. The original members of the Banter Channel were Liam Prentice, Narubate Vaughan, Tom Hall and Nick Cook.
