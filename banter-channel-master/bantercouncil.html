What is the Banter Council?

The Banter Council is an administrative branch of the Banter Channel. It was founded to address conflict between members of the main Banter Channel group.
Since then, the members whose the Council's task was in moderating have disbanded. The Council's role now is now strictly traditional, and rather a term of the collective three members which led the Council. It has also been known as the Banter Caliphate.
The Banter Council is responsible for the management, and security of the Banter Channel's Banter Trust
